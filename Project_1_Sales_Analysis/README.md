# Sales Performance Analysis (Revenue Focused)

## Problem Statement
The objective of this project is to analyze sales data to understand overall performance, identify trends, and provide actionable business insights.

## Business Questions
- What is the overall sales performance?
- Which categories and sub-categories generate the most sales?
- Which regions and cities contribute the highest sales?
- How do sales trends change over time?
- Which customer segments drive the most revenue?

## Tools Used
- Excel

## Dataset
The dataset contains order-level sales data including Order ID, Order Date, Ship Date, Ship Mode, Customer ID, Customer Name, Segment, Country, City, State, Postal Code, Region, Product ID, Category, Sub-Category, Product Name, and Sales.

## Key Insights
- **Top Category & Sub-Category:** Technology generates the highest sales among all categories, with Phones being the top-performing sub-category.  
- **Highest Revenue Region:** The West region drives the most revenue overall.  
- **Seasonal Trends:** Sales peak during September, November, and December, indicating strong seasonal demand.  
- **Top Customer Segment:** The Consumer segment contributes the largest portion of total sales.

## Recommendations
- Focus marketing campaigns on the Technology category and Phones sub-category to maximize revenue.  
- Plan inventory and promotions ahead of peak months (September, November, December) to meet seasonal demand.  
- Strengthen sales strategies in underperforming regions to balance revenue distribution.  
- Target the Consumer segment with retention campaigns and personalized offers to sustain high sales.
